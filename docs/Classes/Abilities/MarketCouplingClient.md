# In short

`MarketCouplingClient` is a client to the [MarketCoupling](../Agents/MarketCoupling.md) agent.

# Available Products

* `TransmissionAndBidForecasts`: Transmission capacities and bids used for a forecast of market coupling
* `TransmissionAndBids`: Transmission capacities and bids used for actual market clearing
